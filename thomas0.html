3. 詳細な添削内容と理由

He saw problem. some people had not food.

→ He saw a problem. Some people had no food.

  • 可算名詞problemには冠詞 "a" が必要。
  • 「食べ物がない」は "had no food" または "did not have food" が一般的。

He had good idea and helped them.

→ He had a good idea and helped them.

  • "idea"も数えられる名詞なので "a" を補う。

In the end every one was happy.

→ In the end, everyone was happy.

  • "In the end"の後のカンマ。
  • "everyone"は1語で書くのが標準的。

We can help world!!

→ We can help the world!!

  • 「世界」を指す場合は通常 "the world" とする。

4. 添削した正答例

Percy and the Quest for "No Hunger"

One day, Percy was in a small town. He saw a problem: some people had no food. He felt very sad. He said, "People need food." He had a good idea and helped them. He brought food and taught them how to grow vegetables. In the end, everyone was happy. We can help the world!!
